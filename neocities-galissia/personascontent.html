>> Starry Skribbles <<

toyhouse profile

Starry Skribbles is my beloved ponysona <3

She runs an art studio in downtown Canterlot where she offers weekly lessons & events! She is roommates with my second ponysona, Shutter Sprocket. Starry represents my passion for art and illustration (as well as the nighttime & the stars) while Shutter Sprocket represents my love of filmmaking.

Starry has been with me for over ten years & has gone through a lot of changes alongside me as I grew up. her original name was Acryllic Heart and she at some point used to be an alicorn as well!




>> Camden Leeds <<

toyhouse profile

Camden is my newest oc, and I made her to replace (other) Jay as my new fursona as I didnt feel as much of a connection with her anymore. sorry jay :(

I decided to make her a Jersey Devil since I've always loved the legend. It was a big part of my childhood, having grown up in the NJ Pine Barrens where the Jersey Devil is said to roam. (i've even visited the house that he was allegedly born in down in Leeds Point!)

Her name is a refence to the city of Camden (used to be considered one of the most dangerous cities on the east coast, and is often what people think of when they hear New Jersey , booooo), and the last name of Mother Leeds, the woman said to have birthed the Jersey Devil!
