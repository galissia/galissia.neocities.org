Who is Moonswirl?

Moonswirl is my first ever oc! She is a pegasus from Canterlot, living in Ponyville with her older sister Midnight. She is the youngest of four and the daughter of Princess Artemis, who herself is the spell-born daughter of Princess Luna.

She works as a weatherpony, particularly with cloud management. She often works the night shift with the help of her night vision, a remnant of her bat pony ancestry (her paternal grandmother was one!).

Moonswirl is a very confident and self-assured mare, sometimes even seeming arrogant at times. She has a stubborn streak and is loyal to a fault. She'd do anything to help her friends. As far as family is concerned, she is much more lax, but still loves them dearly. She rarely mentions her royal status (Lady) but is not averse to occasionally using it to get her way. She has a passion for knitting and an interest in dream interpretation.

She travels through time and space with her childhood friends Azalea Blossom and Time Warp as they search for TW's missing parents, Doctor Whooves and Derpy Hooves. On one trip, they bring Midnight along with them as they need her assistance in travelling into Nightmare Moon's dreams. While there, a piece of the Tantabus ends up latching onto Midnight and travelling back to the present with her, eventually festering and assisting her transformation into her Nightmare form.

A good chunk of her story is still in progess and has yet to be written.

Moonswirl was created in 2013.

I was 9, and she was my first ever original character. She was originally supposed to be me as a pony, my first ponysona before I really knew what 'ponysona' meant.

Gradually, though, she started to grow farther away from me as a person and more towards being her own character. I then created Starry Skribbles (then known as Acryllic Heart) as a replacement and a proper ponysona!

Moonswirl remained as my main character and mascot online. Most if not all my usernames had 'Moonswirl' in it in one way or another!

"The Story of Moonswirl"

Somewhere along the line, I decided I'd give a shot at writing some sort of story for her. What had been intended as a backstory ultimately turned out to be a day-in-the-life story and I never got around to expanding it.

For years, I actually thought I had lost the original for good, but when I was cleaning my room I happened to find a printed copy tucked in one of my old sketchbooks! (thank god!) so here it is, complete with the original pony creator images :)
