    Hello hello hello!! welcome to my cozy lil' corner of the internet where I can safely hide away from the rest of society. i made this site mainly because I found some cool neocities sites and wanted to make my own and practice my HTML in the process.

    This website is largely experimental and made of bits and pieces of code I pulled from other sites, with my own work peppered throughout. Mostly, this is a self-indulgent internet space, so i don't expect everyone to find anything useful here, but if someone can find something neat and interesting here, or otherwise enjoy my ramblings, that'd be really awesome :)

    be sure to check out my sitemap! not all my pages are listed in the nav boxes :)

    this website contains autoplay music! it is not optimized for mobile and i don't plan on making it so! there should not be any super severe issues if you choose to view on a phone, but this was meant to display best on a 1920 x 1080 desktop. ^^
