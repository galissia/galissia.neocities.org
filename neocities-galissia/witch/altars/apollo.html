APOLLO

Απολλων

greek god of the sun, poetry, music, archery, prophecy, disease, healing

Apollo was the ancient Greek god of music and poetry and younger brother of Artemis. He is one of the twelve Olympians and is the son of Zeus and Leto.

He pulls the sun across the sky in a chariot led by four horses and is often conflated with Helios because of this.

Him and Artemis are both deities of disease and sudden death; she targets girls and women while he targets boys and men. He is a protector of boys and young men as well.
